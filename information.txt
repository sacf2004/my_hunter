MY HUNTER

History:

My Hunter is a game created by nintendo, in fact it was one of their first 
games. It was made by Andres Sebastian Sanchez Cortez, in 1984. 

What it is about:

The hunter is a game about hunting as many ducks as you can. 

Actions:

- If the duck disappears nothing happens it will reappear soon after.
- To hunt a duck you must click with the mouse.
- You can close the tab by clicking the close button.
